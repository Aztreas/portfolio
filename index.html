A Little Bit About Me


I am an undergraduate student at the University of California, Berkeley studying Computer and Data Science with interests in full stack development, fintech, and machine learning. I love to learn anything and everything.

This summer, I worked with Fidelity Investments as a Software Engineering Intern, focusing on UI/UX testing and app development. I have also built software as a member of the Zimmerbot team for Wikipedia , and conducted research on effective police responses as part of the DecidingForce project. Currently, I am an active equity research analyst for the Berkeley Investment Group, a consultant for DiversaTech Consulting, and a lab assistant for the Data 8 course (Fundamentals of Data Science) at UC Berkeley.

Feel free to take a look at some of my work, or reach out at ericli3088@berkeley.edu
